The Designer

An editorial eye
trained across two
decades of design.

Before flowers, there was fashion. Before fashion, there was art. Jo's path to floral design was never straight — it was intentional, winding through interior design, styling, and a deep study of what makes a space feel alive.

That background lives in every arrangement. The scale, the tension between color and restraint, the instinct to pull materials that feel unexpected together — all of it comes from years of working at the intersection of aesthetics and purpose.

Jo founded Modern LUXE Flowers to bring a different standard to Chicago. Not just beautiful flowers — but a creative process clients actually trust.
